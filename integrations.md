# Integrations

Connect your accounts so Lane can publish, measure, and advertise on your behalf. Everything happens in your brand's workspace under **Integrations**.

**Your credentials are safe with us.** Access tokens and API keys are encrypted at rest, never shared with AI agents, never logged, and never stored in plain text. You can disconnect any integration at any time from the same screen.

## Social channels

Lane drafts and publishes posts to the channels you connect — always through your approval queue first.

- **LinkedIn** — connect with LinkedIn sign-in (OAuth). Lane can post to your profile; organization pages can be configured after connecting.
- **Facebook** — connect with Facebook sign-in, then pick the Page Lane should publish to. You can change the selected Page later without reconnecting.
- **Instagram** — connect with Instagram sign-in (a professional account linked to a Facebook Page is required by Meta). Lane can publish feed posts, reels, and stories.
- **X (Twitter)** — connect with your own X developer app credentials (Consumer Key, Consumer Secret, Access Token, and Access Token Secret from the X Developer Portal). Make sure the app has Read and Write permissions.
- **Bluesky** — connect with your Bluesky handle and an app password (created in Bluesky under Settings → App passwords).
- **YouTube** — connect your YouTube channel for video-oriented campaigns.

## Analytics

Connect these so Lane can see what's working and let results shape its proposals.

- **Google Analytics 4** — connect with Google sign-in and pick your GA4 property.
- **Google Search Console** — connect with Google sign-in and pick your verified site.

## Advertising

- **Google Ads** — connect with Google sign-in and choose your ads account. Lane can draft Demand Gen and Search campaigns for your review; nothing launches without your approval.

## Content platform

- **Sanity** — connect your Sanity CMS with a Project ID, Dataset, API version, and an API token (create one with Editor+ permissions in Sanity Manage). The token is encrypted at rest and never displayed after it's stored.

## Tips

- Start with the channels you actually use — you can add more any time.
- If a connection expires (tokens do), the Integrations screen will tell you; reconnecting takes a minute.
- Analytics connections are read-only signals for Lane. Publishing always goes through your approval queue.
