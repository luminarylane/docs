# Luminary Lane Documentation

Luminary Lane is your AI marketing team. Lane — your AI CMO — studies your brand, watches your market, and drafts campaigns, posts, and outreach for you to review. Every proposal waits for your approval before anything is published or sent. Nothing ships without you.

## Quick start

1. **Sign up and pick a plan** — each plan is a weekly budget of Lane Cycles (see [Account & billing](account-and-billing.md)).
2. **Set your brand in motion** — confirm what Lane learned from your website and ask your first question (see [Brand onboarding](brand-onboarding.md)).
3. **Connect your channels** — link LinkedIn, Instagram, Google Analytics, and more so Lane can publish and measure on your behalf (see [Integrations](integrations.md)).
