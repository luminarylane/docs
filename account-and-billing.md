# Account & billing

## Plans

Every plan is a weekly budget of **Lane Cycles** — the operating passes Lane runs on your brand. A Cycle is a full working session: reading signals, drafting answers to your questions, and preparing proposals for your approval.

| Plan | Price | Active spaces (brands) | Lane Cycles per week | Max Cycles per space per week |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Discover** | $100/mo | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| **Growth** | $500/mo | 5 | 5 | 2 |
| **Scale** | $1,500/mo | 15 | 15 | 3 |
| **Agency** | $3,500/mo | 35 | 35 | 5 |

You can change plans any time from **Profile → Billing → Change plan**.

## How weekly Cycles work

- Your Cycle budget runs on a **weekly window: Monday 00:00 to the next Monday 00:00 in your timezone**. A fresh budget opens every Monday.
- Cycles don't roll over — each week starts clean.
- You can see how many Cycles you have left this week in the **header** (on any brand page) and in **Profile → Billing**.
- When you've used all your Cycles for the week, Lane pauses scheduled runs until the fresh budget opens Monday. Anything already queued picks up in the new week.

## External operating budget

Your plan covers Lane's Cycles — the analysis, planning, and drafting. **External spend** (AI models, media generation, email sending, data, and ads) is a separate operating budget that you approve and control. Lane never spends it without your approval, and it is never billed as Cycle overage.

## Founding offer

During our launch window: **50% off your first month**, then the published monthly price unless you cancel. One per account. A card is required — this is a discounted paid subscription, not a free trial.

## Managing billing

Payments are processed by Stripe — we never store your card details. From **Profile → Billing** you can:

- **Manage billing** — open the Stripe portal to update your card, download invoices, or cancel
- **Change plan** — move between Discover, Growth, Scale, and Agency

If you cancel, your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period.
